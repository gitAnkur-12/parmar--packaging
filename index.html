About Parmar Packaging

Parmar Packaging is a leading manufacturer and wholesale supplier of plastic packaging materials based in Bhiwandi. With over 25 years of experience, we offer 50+ packaging products including LD bags, HM bags, PP woven bags, BOPP bags, stretch film, shrink wrap, courier bags, tapes, bubble wrap and more.

We specialize in custom printing, bulk pricing and fast delivery for manufacturers, traders, exporters, retailers and industrial clients across India.

Whether you need standard supplies or customized packaging, Parmar Packaging guarantees quality, speed, and reliability.
